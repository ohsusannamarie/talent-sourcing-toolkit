# Intake Questions Template

Use this during recruiter intake, hiring manager calibration, or role reset conversations.

---

## Role basics

- What is the official job title?
- What title would candidates actually use for this kind of work?
- What team is this role on?
- Who does this person report to?
- Is this a new role or backfill?
- What problem is this person being hired to solve?

---

## Must-haves

- What are the true non-negotiables?
- Which skills must they have on day one?
- What experience can be learned after joining?
- What would make someone an immediate no?
- What requirements are flexible for an exceptional candidate?

---

## Technical context

- What tools, technologies, platforms, or systems will they use?
- Which technologies are required vs preferred?
- What adjacent skills could translate well?
- What kind of project complexity should they have seen before?

---

## Target background

- Which companies produce strong talent for this role?
- Which industries are most relevant?
- Are there companies or backgrounds to avoid?
- What career path usually prepares someone for this work?

---

## Selling points

- Why would someone want this role?
- What is exciting about the team, product, mission, or technical challenge?
- What can this person learn or own?
- What should we say to candidates who are not actively looking?

---

## Process and calibration

- What does a strong resume look like?
- What does a weak-but-possible resume look like?
- Can you share example profiles?
- Who is involved in interviews?
- What are the most common reasons candidates fail the process?
- What does success look like in the first 6-12 months?
