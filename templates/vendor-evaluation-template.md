## Risk review

- Does it handle candidate or personal data?
- Does it connect to email, LinkedIn, ATS, CRM, or calendar tools?
- Does it use AI for screening, ranking, scoring, or recommendations?
- Does it require browser permissions?
- Is there a privacy policy?
- Is vendor ownership clear?
- Would security/legal need to review this?
