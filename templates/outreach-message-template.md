# Outreach Message Template

Use this as a flexible starting point. The goal is not to sound templated. The goal is to avoid starting from a blank page.

---

## Short candidate outreach

```txt
Hi [Name],

I came across your background in [specific relevant area]. I am working on a [role/team/problem] where your experience with [specific skill/domain/project] looks especially relevant.

Would you be open to a quick conversation about it?
```

---

## Slightly warmer version

```txt
Hi [Name],

I came across your work/background in [specific area] and wanted to reach out directly. I am supporting a [role] focused on [specific problem/team/product], and your experience with [specific skill/domain] stood out as relevant.

Would you be open to hearing more?
```

---

## Follow-up

```txt
Hi [Name],

Just wanted to follow up once in case this got buried. I thought your experience with [specific area] could be relevant to a [role/team] I am supporting.

No worries if the timing is not right - happy to share details if useful.
```

---

## Quick quality check

Before sending, ask:

- Is it clear why I am contacting this person?
- Is the message specific without being creepy?
- Is the role understandable?
- Is the ask easy to answer?
- Did I remove filler and fake excitement?
