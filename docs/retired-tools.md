## How an entry lands here

When a tool dies, is acquired into oblivion, or becomes unsafe:

1. Set `status: sunset` on its entry in `data/site.yaml` (instead of deleting it), and
   add a short `sunset_note`.
2. Move its row into this file with what happened and any replacement.
3. The active library and site drop it automatically; the history survives here.

This is the "sunset graveyard" from the roadmap — retirement with a paper trail, not a
silent delete.
